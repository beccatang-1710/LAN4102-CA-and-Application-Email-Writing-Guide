Salutation / 稱謂
Dear Sir/ Madam,
Dear Ms/ Mrs/ Mr XXXX, 		敬啟者，
Tip: Use Dear Sir/ Madam when you do not know the recipient's name. Use Dear Ms/ Mrs/ Mr [Last Name] when you do know their name — this is more personal and preferred. "Ms" is safe regardless of marital status; use "Mrs" only if the recipient explicitly uses it.
Opening (choose either A or B) / 開頭段
A. I am writing to express my interest in the position of [job title]A as advertised on [job board/website]A/B dated [date of the advertisement]A. As a [year X/final-year]A/B Higher Diploma student in [study programme]A/B at the Hong Kong Institute of Information Technology (HKIIT) at IVE [(campus)]A/B, I am eager to utilise my skills in [a relevant subject area]A to meet the unique needs of your customers/clients. 本人謹此表達對貴公司於 [招聘平台/網站] [廣告日期] 刊登之 [職位名稱] 一職的興趣。本人現就讀於香港專業教育學院 (IVE) [(校園)] 香港資訊科技學院 (HKIIT) [課程名稱] 高級文憑課程（[第X年/最後一年]），渴望能運用本人在 [相關學科領域] 的技能，滿足貴公司客戶的獨特需要。
B. With reference to the advertisement on [job board/website]A/B dated [date]B, I am writing to apply for the position of [job title]B. As a [year X/final-year]A/B Higher Diploma student in [study programme]A/B at the Hong Kong Institute of Information Technology (HKIIT) at IVE [(campus)]A/B, I am eager to contribute to your company with my knowledge of [a relevant subject area]B and strong passion for [the industry]B. 參閱 [招聘平台/網站] [日期] 所刊登之廣告，本人謹此申請 [職位名稱] 一職。本人現就讀於香港專業教育學院 (IVE) [(校園)] 香港資訊科技學院 (HKIIT) [課程名稱] 高級文憑課程（[第X年/最後一年]），期望憑藉本人在 [相關學科領域] 的知識及對 [行業] 的熱忱，為貴公司作出貢獻。

As a Year 2 Higher Diploma student in Games and Animation at the Hong Kong Institute of Information Technology (HKIIT) at IVE (Lee Wai Lee), I am eager to utilise my knowledge of gaming technology and skills in graphics programming to meet the needs of your clients. 範例（資訊科技）：本人謹此表達對貴公司於 GoodjobHK 2023年9月4日刊登之遊戲開發員職位的興趣。本人現就讀於香港專業教育學院 (IVE)（李惠利）香港資訊科技學院 (HKIIT) 遊戲及動畫高級文憑課程（第二年），渴望能運用本人在遊戲技術方面的知識及圖形編程技能，滿足貴公司客戶的需要。
Example (DE): I am writing to express my interest in the position of jewellery designer-maker as advertised on GoodjobHK dated 5 September 2024. As a Year 2 Higher Diploma student in Jewellery Design and Technology at the Hong Kong Design Institute (HKDI), I am eager to utilise my jewellery design skills and jewellery-making techniques to contribute to your company. 範例（設計）：本人謹此表達對貴公司於 GoodjobHK 2024年9月5日刊登之珠寶設計師兼製作師職位的興趣。本人現就讀於香港知專設計學院 (HKDI) 珠寶設計及科技高級文憑課程（第二年），渴望能運用本人的珠寶設計技巧及珠寶製作技術，為貴公司作出貢獻。
Para 2–3 (Main Body) – Demonstrating your skills with evidence (one paragraph about education while another about previous work experience) / 第2–3段（主體段落）— 以事例展示技能

As a [job title/position]M, I have strengthened/sharpened my [skill]M. 擔任 [職位／職銜] 期間，本人已強化／磨練了本人的 [技能]。
My experience in [X industry/job nature, e.g., customer service/handling complaints]M has allowed/enabled/helped me to [a job responsibility from the job ad]M. 本人在 [X行業／工作性質，例如：客戶服務／處理投訴] 方面的經驗，讓本人能夠 [招聘廣告中的工作職責]。
With my academic background in [name of your HD programme, e.g., AI and Smart Technology]M, I am confident that I would be a great addition to your company. 憑藉本人在 [高級文憑課程名稱，例如：人工智能及智慧科技] 的學術背景，本人深信自己將成為貴公司的寶貴人才。
With my relevant experience in [X industry/job nature, e.g., customer service/handling complaints]M, I am confident that I would be a great addition to your company/organisation. 憑藉本人在 [X行業／工作性質，例如：客戶服務／處理投訴] 方面的相關經驗，本人深信自己將成為貴公司／機構的寶貴人才。
During my studies/internship/industrial attachment at [school/company name]M, I sharpened my [skill]M by [verb+ing]M … 在 [學校／公司名稱] 就讀／實習期間，本人透過 [動詞+ing] 磨練了本人的 [技能]……
As a [job title/position]M, I strengthened my [skill]M through [verb+ing]M … 擔任 [職位／職銜] 期間，本人透過 [動詞+ing] 強化了本人的 [技能]……
I …, which enabled me to strengthen/sharpen my [skill]M. 本人……，此經歷讓本人得以強化／磨練 [技能]。
I completed [courses/projects]M, which enhanced/improved my [skills]M. 本人完成了 [課程／項目]，從而提升了本人的 [技能]。
Example (IT) — Para 2 (Education): During my studies, I completed a project about the application of virtual reality (VR) technology in a fitness studio, which strengthened my understanding of the principles and techniques of creating an immersive and interactive gaming experience. Furthermore, I completed a course, titled Virtual and Augmented Reality Studies, which enhanced my time management and my communication skills.

During this experience, I sharpened my teamwork skills by collaborating with colleagues from various departments to meet customers' needs. As the vice president of the Gaming Society at HKIIT, I further developed my passion for introducing the latest gaming technology to our members through organising regular meet-ups.

Furthermore, I completed a course titled Jewellery Business: Branding and Marketing, which improved my time management and my attention to details. Sign-off / 署名
Yours faithfully,
[Full name] e.g. Clayton Chan

OR

Yours sincerely,
[Full name] e.g. Clayton Chan 		謹上，
（全名）例：Clayton Chan
Tip: Use Yours faithfully when you do not know the recipient's name (i.e. you began with Dear Sir/ Madam). Use Yours sincerely when you do know their name (i.e. you began with Dear Ms/ Mrs/ Mr [Name]). Match the salutation and sign-off consistently.

提示：如你不知道收件人姓名（即開首用 Dear Sir/ Madam），則用 Yours faithfully。如你知道對方姓名（即開首用 Dear Ms/ Mrs/ Mr [姓名]），則用 Yours sincerely。稱謂與署名須保持一致。
